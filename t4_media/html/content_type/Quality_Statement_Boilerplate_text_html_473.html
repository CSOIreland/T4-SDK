Quality Statement

These official statistics are compiled by the CSO in accordance with the principles of the European Statistics Code of Practice (ES CoP) which means statistics are designed to:

  • meet identified user needs
  • be well explained and readily accessible
  • be accurate, reliable and coherent, using sound methods
  • be managed impartially and objectively in the public interest

Further information on the methods used and the strengths and limitations of the data are available in the Methods Page.
